🏀 Vision Brief · for Benson

What if every athlete
carried your coaching
in their pocket?

Level Up already connects you to your players between sessions. This is the plan to put a piece of your coaching brain - your drills, your cues, your eye for form, your encouragement - into the app, so it shows up for every athlete, every rep, even when you can't be there.

AI doesn't replace the coach. It gives the coach reach.

The one idea behind all of this

You can only be in one gym at a time.

A great coach is a bottleneck - and that's a good problem, because it means the coaching is worth wanting. But it caps how many athletes you can truly reach, and it means the same question gets answered for the 40th time, the same beginner workout gets written from scratch, the same bent-back squat gets corrected in person or not at all.

Everything in this document is one move: take the things that don't actually need you in the room, and let the app carry them - in your voice, with your standards - so your real time goes to the things that do need you. We are not adding an AI gimmick. We are subtracting repetitive work from your day and multiplying your presence in your athletes' training.

The test for every feature here

Does it remove a task a human is doing today, or does it just add a new AI-flavoured task? We only build the first kind. If it doesn't give you back time or give an athlete coaching they wouldn't otherwise get, it doesn't ship.

A day, before and after

What this actually feels like

Today

Marcus messages at 9pm: "what should I work on this week?" You see it tomorrow afternoon, write a workout from scratch, copy three drills you've used before, type out the cues you've typed a hundred times. Meanwhile his form video from Tuesday is still unwatched in your queue with eleven others.

With Level Up AI

Marcus's companion answers at 9:01pm in your style, and drafts a shooting block from your library. You get a notification: "Draft ready for Marcus - approve?" One tap. His Tuesday video already has instant form feedback; the app flagged only the two clips where something looked genuinely off, and queued them at the top for your eyes.

Same coaching. Same standards. You made every real decision. But the typing, the assembling, the first-pass triage, and the after-hours "you're doing great, keep going" - the app carried all of it.

Why this is achievable, not a moonshot

Level Up is already shaped for this

The hard infrastructure is built. These features connect things that already exist in the app today.

💬 Chat is live

Coaches and athletes already message - text and video, with read receipts. An AI companion is a new voice in a pipe that already works, not a new system.

🎥 Form videos already flow

Athletes already record and upload form-check videos to the app. The pose-coaching feature already has its input and the user habit - we add the eye.

📋 Workouts are structured

Workouts and drills are stored as clean, structured data - sets, reps, video demos, coach notes. That's a ready-made library for AI to search and to build from.

Bottom line

We're not rebuilding Level Up. We're adding a brain to a body that's already standing up and moving.

The path, not the cliff

We build it in waves

Each wave ships something useful on its own, and earns the right to build the next. You'll feel value early, long before the full vision lands.

Wave 1 · Now

Capture & assist

Coach knowledge base + a chat companion that answers from it and drafts your replies. Cheapest to build, fastest to feel.

Wave 2 · Next

Generate & act

AI drafts workouts and handles assignments on your say-so. Your library becomes a creative engine.

Wave 3 · North star

See & correct

On-device form analysis on every rep, with you in the loop for the calls that matter. The flagship.

The rules we hold ourselves to

Five principles

  • The coach is never replaced. Every AI output is a draft, a first pass, or a hype-up - the real coaching calls stay with Benson. AI does the reps, not the judgement.
  • It must subtract work. Each feature removes a task you do today. If it only adds an AI chore, we don't build it.
  • It speaks in your voice. The companion answers from your knowledge, your drills, your cues - not generic internet fitness advice.
  • Privacy is a feature, not a footnote. Form video is analysed on the athlete's phone wherever possible - the raw video never has to leave the device. Especially important with young athletes. See how →
  • You stay in the loop. Anything the app does on your behalf - sending, assigning, generating - you see and approve. No surprises sent in your name.
